Initial release: managed redirects for Payload with a cache-backed Next.js middleware matcher. Orderable redirects collection with internal-document or custom-URL destinations, regex matching with capture-group substitution, scroll-to anchors appended as `#fragments`, background hit tracking, and pluggable caches — Vercel Runtime Cache (with a composable development cache), JSON file, or in-memory. Destination collections re-sync the cache automatically when a published document moves or is deleted.
